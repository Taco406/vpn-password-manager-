## Name

SENTINEL

## Short description (≤132 chars)

Autofill logins from your local SENTINEL vault. Zero-knowledge: nothing leaves your device.

## Detailed description

SENTINEL fills the logins you've saved in the SENTINEL desktop app straight into Chrome.

- **Local-only.** The extension talks to the SENTINEL app on your own computer over Chrome's native
  messaging — there is no SENTINEL server and nothing is uploaded anywhere.
- **You stay in control.** A website only ever receives the credentials you saved for that exact
  site, and only while your vault is unlocked. Lock the vault and autofill goes dark.
- **No tracking.** No analytics, no ads, no accounts.

Requires the free SENTINEL desktop app, which holds your encrypted vault and generates and stores
your passwords.
